Nucleagg

Nucleagg is a game I developed for the EmTech course at Ghent University.
This is done in the P5.js library for JavaScript.
Every aspect of this game is programmed by myself.

Nucleagg is meant to teach people the safety of nuclear powerplants.
The media has portrayed nuclear power as a threat to humanity while it's actually the safest to generate energy.
Nuclear energy is not even at it's final form, it can be improved to unheard levels through the use of Thorium reactors. These do need more research and funding however.
Still not convinced? Nuclear energy is green.
